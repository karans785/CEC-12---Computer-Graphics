OVERVIEW:

This program clips the line segment using the MidPoint Subdivision line clipping algorithm.

INSTRUCTIONS: 

1.Kindly click on the .exe file (application)  mid.exe .

2.You will see the clipping window and line segment in RED color.

3.Now press 'c' on the keyboard to clip the line.

4.You can see the line being clipped (in Yellow color).
